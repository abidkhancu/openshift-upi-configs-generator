OpenShift UPI Config Generator

Usage Instructions

After generating the files, copy them to the appropriate locations on your system:

  • named.conf → /etc/named.conf or /var/named/named.conf
  • Forward and Reverse Zones → /var/named/
  • haproxy.cfg → /etc/haproxy/haproxy.cfg or /var/haproxy/haproxy.cfg

Ensure the ownership and permissions are correct for the services to read them:

chown root:named /var/named/*
chmod 640 /var/named/*
chown haproxy:haproxy /var/haproxy/haproxy.cfg
chmod 640 /var/haproxy/haproxy.cfg
